Instructor: Prof. Matthew Choptuik

Homework 1 & 2:

Solving vaccum Einstein equations in plane symmetry using BSSN and Generalized Harmonic Formulation

  • Notes on Gravitational Waves Using BSSN and Generalized Harmonic Formulation : In this note I cast the Einstein vacuum equations in BSSN and generalized harmonic decomposition. It is restricted to planar symmetry, while we have two space-like killing vectors. All of the equations of motion are written explicitly. Methods for solving and verifying the solution are described in the note (This note is under construction at the moment, June 07, 2010)

Einstein-Vlasov system, study of critical collapse for massive and massless case
